Game Description

You play as Bloom, a pink-haired girl with butterfly wings and the ability to fire Love at everything. Fly into [space] and shoot down some monsters! Goes up to ten (10) levels with a boss at the end.

About

Cute little shmup I put together in a couple of days (way longer than it should have). Besides testing out game mechanics, I wanted to try using singletons and classes for a project in Unity. Due to my lack of exposure to singletons, especially on Unity, the code ended up being pretty messy, though it was still a good learning experience.
