Game Description

Help the Eyeworm improve its vision! Collect "eyedrops" to allow the Eyeworm to "see" and collect more letters in the map. Gather every letter to complete the vision exam chart and unlock the exit!

About

A short game made under 24 hours for the RDC 2020 Game Jam. The theme for the jam was "2020 Vision". At first, I wanted to create a horror game, hence the amount of darkness in the game. Since I worked alone for this jam however, I decided the initial ideas I had in mind were too ambitious and simplified them.
