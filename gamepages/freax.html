Game Description

[Project still in progress.] An open-arena creature game where you take the role as one of many originally designed creatures, also known as Freax(tentative). Each creature has their own unique moveset and stats, as well as varying costumes! Battle other players or just hang out!

About

A simple multiplayer action game where players are free to play the game how they want. The game offers options to duke it out with other players or simply hang out, all while taking the role as original, unique creatures. Scripted with Lua in an object-oriented style using Roblox Studio.
