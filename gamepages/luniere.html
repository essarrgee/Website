Game Description

A short, horror-themed adventure game with a paper aesthetic.

Luniere wants to go back home, but the door is locked, and the keys are missing! Help her gather the keys and make it back home safe and sound! But beware; who knows what may lurk in the dark at night?

About

Winning 1st place in Aesthetics, Luniere made for Scream Jam 2020 in under 10 days with teammate Branchi, who was responsible for all the art. Tools used included Unity, Blender, and Photoshop.
