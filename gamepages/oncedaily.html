Game Description

A cute, simple puzzle game. You've been feeling sick for a while. Maybe a simple trip to the hospital will help?

About

A game made for itch.io's Scream Jam 2019. The game was developed with Unity and the graphics were created with MagicaVoxel. I joined the jam late, so I only had around three days to work on this game. The theme of the jam was of course, horror, but due to time constraints and poor planning, I couldn't quite capture the horror "feel" for the game. Nonetheless, I tried to include an underlying message in this game, in a similar vain to Bloom, another game I've worked on.
