Polluted Flesh

Image Gallery

Game Description

A very short, on-rails collector game where you dodge obstacles and collect floating waste inside a monster. Features 2 endings.

About

This game was made with a simple concept in mind. The point of it was to practice how much I could polish up a game. I also used this as an opportunity to test out a Playstation 1 style for graphics.
