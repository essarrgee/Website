Game Description

A short puzzle/horror game.

Following the events of Luniere, our protagonist and her friend, Solaire, have entered Pengu's Manor to spend some quality time with their friend Pengu. Things don't go as planned, however, and it's up to our protagonists to save the day!

About

Second Manor was made for itch.io's Scream Jam 2021 in under 10 days with teammate Branchi, who was responsible for all the art. Tools used included Unity, Blender, and Photoshop.
