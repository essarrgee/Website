Game Description

Quill got separated from his sister and got lost! Will he survive or will he be swallowed whole by the labyrinth?

A horror adventure game following the narrative of a boy named Quill and his sister. Guide Quill as he endures through five (5) nights of frights and oddities. Collect secrets along the way to uncover more about the story!

About

A short horror adventure game made using Bloom as the foundation. Demonstrates a more direct, story-driven style. Scripted with Lua in an object-oriented style using Roblox Studio.
