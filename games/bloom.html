Bloom

Game Description

Oh no! You've locked yourself inside! Is there any way out?
A short Puzzle/Adventure game with cute visuals. Help your character finally escape her room by collecting and making use of items scattered around! Discover 5 endings, each with their own badge achievement!

About

A puzzle/adventure game made in Roblox with simplistic controls in mind. Gameplay length is short and sweet but contains an underlying message to players. Scripted with Lua in an object-oriented style using Roblox Studio.
