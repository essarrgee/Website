Dodge the Blocks!

Game Description

Dodge incoming blocks to increase your score! How long can you survive?

About

A game made under 2 hours to practice for Ludum Dare 45. Prior to this project, I only played around with Unity to make a simple character move around in 3D space. This game was the result of cramming a ton of documentation and tutorials to understand how most of Unity works.
